Epic Fight Arena is a PvP-focused Minecraft server built around the Epic Fight mod, which overhauls Minecraftâ€™s combat mechanics by adding complex fighting styles and animations. The mod brings a more action-oriented, "soulslike" combat experience.

In our server players can engage in intense PvP battles using different classes, special attacks, and unique abilities.

The server itself offers various game modes, including KitPvP, where players can pick pre-set kits with unique gear and abilities, Battle Royale, King of The Hill, and many more.

It's tailored for players who enjoy tactical combat with additional depth, from managing stamina during battles to utilizing different fighting strategies depending on their class or weapons.
Frequently asked questions
What is EFA?
arrow_downward
EFA is an Epic Fight mod PvP server, with many game modes and mini-games, similar to Hypixel, EFA is built from the ground up with 100% original assets and code. No plugins or additional mods, only Epic Fight with our custom integrated datapacks which make everything work.
Is the server 24/7?
arrow_downward
Yes, the server is and always will be online 24/7!
Why can't I see my skin?
arrow_downward
The server is set to offline mode, meaning players without official Minecraft accounts are able to join. This also means you cannot see skins, however the Offline Skins mod fixes this issue! See Here

We made this decision due to a major number of our players being offline users, we wouldn't want to leave them out.
Will there be American/Asian servers?
arrow_downward
Yes, we are currently working on expanding our servers, however we are still quite small and it's difficult financially managing several servers at once.
